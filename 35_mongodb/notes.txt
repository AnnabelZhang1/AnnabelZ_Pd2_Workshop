- MongoDB is a NoSql database, and rather store info in documents 
- Document: a way ot organize and store data as a set of field-value pairs
- Collection: an organized store of documents in mongodb, usually with common fields between documents
- Field: a unique identifier for a specific datapoint (each field has a value associated with it)
- Atlas allows the deployment of clusters (a group of servers that store your data)
- Instance: a single macine locally or in te cloud, running) 
- Replica Set: a few connected MongoDB instaces that store the same data (as backup)
